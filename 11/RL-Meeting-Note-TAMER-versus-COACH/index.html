RL Meeting Note: TAMER versus COACH

  • Under policy shaping strategy, the MDP in TAMER trainer’s mind is changing, whereas COACH’s is consistent.
  • Basic TAMER cannot catch the changes with time, the trainer needs to keep giving positive (non-zero and non-negative)feedbacks when the agent behaves expectedly; on the contrary, COACH’s trainer needs to follow the diminishing returns strategy.
