 2018/03/25   Share

What did you do last week?

The past week was just a nightmare. Before the spring break, we were all busy preparing for our midterms, so our group did not make much progress. I expected our group could some spend time working on project 2 but eventually it turned out that everyone was out of town. Therefore, finally, I and Javier had to get everything done in just one week. It is so exhausting.

What is in your way?

Since Austen drops this course due to some reason, professor Downing gave our group a two-day extension. So, I just finished the second phase. But I find that some of our APIs return some useless data, which slow down the loading speed of some web pages. Thus, currently, I working on improving our API.

What will you do next week?

Obviously, I have to take a break in the first two days of next week. Then I might take some time to improve what we fail to do well in this phase, like the Mocha tests and python unit tests. Also, I really hope to start the next phase early and get my group mates more involved.

What is your experience of this class?

Honestly, I feel both good and bad in this class. The good thing is, thanks to my group mates, I’ve become a full stack developer. I and Javier were forced to learn a series of tools and wrote every single code in our project except the unit tests. I am happy that I am able to build a complete website and provide API service, from front-end to backend. However, the bad thing is that the collaboration in our group doesn’t meet my expectation at the beginning of this course. Every time I ask someone other than Javier to work, they always say “I am trying”, “I am out of town”, “I have a test this week”. I think those excuses are all unacceptable. Everyone has several tests throughout the entire semester. I also had a test on Friday when I was working on our project on Thursday night. Both preparing for midterm and working on the project are the responsibility of students like us.

What’s my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?

My tip-of-the-week is “take this course with some guys who you know before this course, who you really want to work with and who you can trust.” Anyone who has read my words above should understand what my tip means.
